Our Story

Founded in 2015, Capella Development Group has established itself as a premier real estate development company, specializing in luxury residential projects that redefine modern living.

Our commitment to excellence, innovative design, and sustainable development practices has earned us recognition as one of the leading developers in the metropolitan area.

Capella Residence represents our flagship project - a testament to our vision of creating homes that blend luxury, comfort, and community in perfect harmony.

Commitment to Sustainability

At Capella Development Group, we recognize our responsibility to the environment and future generations. Our sustainability initiatives include:

  • Energy-efficient building systems and LED lighting
  • Water conservation technologies and rainwater harvesting
  • Use of sustainable and recycled building materials
  • Green spaces and landscaping with native plants
  • LEED certification for all new developments
