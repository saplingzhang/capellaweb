Frequently Asked Questions

What is the expected completion date?

Capella Residence is expected to be completed by Q4 2024, with move-ins beginning in January 2025.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, we welcome pets! There are some restrictions on size and breed. Please inquire about our pet policy for details.

What financing options are available?

We offer construction payment plans and work with major banks to provide competitive mortgage rates. Our team can help with pre-approval.

Can I customize my unit?

Limited customization options are available for units purchased early in the construction phase. Contact us to discuss possibilities.

What warranty is provided?

All units come with a comprehensive 5-year warranty covering structural elements and a 2-year warranty on appliances and finishes.

Is rental investment allowed?

Yes, rental investment is permitted with some restrictions. Please discuss our rental policy with our sales team.
